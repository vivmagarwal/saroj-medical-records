## Notes on extraction & verification

- Dates are taken from the PDF as the date the test was *performed* / sample *collected* (not the report-issue date).
- Titles for imaging come from the `Study Description` field; for lab reports, the primary test/panel name; for summaries/consents, the document type.
- **Summaries are vision-verified**: each report's image was read by Claude and the markdown summary edited to match the PDF content. Lab summaries include actual values, units, reference ranges, and abnormality flags.
- Same-day duplicate tests are disambiguated with a `_2` suffix in the filename.
- Verbatim PDF text (deterministic, character-accurate from `pdfplumber`) is preserved in `report_md/<name>.md` under `## Verbatim text from PDF`.
- Each per-report markdown records its `**Original filename**` (the hospital-issued `MedicalReport_*.pdf` name) for reference and reversibility.
