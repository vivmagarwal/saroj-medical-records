## Summary (extracted)

Department of Laboratory Medicine - Microbiology. Urine Culture Colony Count - Final report (Foley's catheter urine; collected 20-Feb-2026 05:17). Clinical details: Breathlessness.

Microscopy (wet mount): Pus cells >100/hpf; RBCs >100/hpf; Epithelial cells 0-5/hpf; Bacteria present.

Culture: Two organisms isolated with significant growth:
1. Enterococcus faecium - Colony count >10^5 CFU/mL (significant bacteriuria)
2. Candida tropicalis - Colony count >10^5 CFU/mL (significant growth)

Drug Susceptibility - Candida tropicalis: Susceptible to Fluconazole (MIC 1), Voriconazole (<=0.12), Caspofungin (<=0.12), Micafungin (<=0.06), Amphotericin B (0.5).

Drug Susceptibility - Enterococcus faecium: Resistant to Ampicillin, Benzylpenicillin (>=64), Ciprofloxacin (>=8), Levofloxacin (>=8), Nitrofurantoin (256), Tetracycline (>=16). Susceptible-Dose Dependent to Daptomycin (1.5). Susceptible to Linezolid (2.0), Teicoplanin (<=0.5), Vancomycin (<=0.5).

Note: E. faecium is inherently resistant to cephalosporins, aminoglycosides, clindamycin, trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole, and fusidic acid. Findings consistent with complicated UTI / urinary fungal infection.
