## Summary (extracted)

18F-FDG WB PET-CT Scan
Clinical History: 63-year-old female, presented with complaints of breathlessness,
productive cough, bilateral pedal edema for the past 2 months; persisting hematuria since
12/02/26. Chest X Ray showed left sided pleural effusion. ICD was inserted (12/02/26),
pleural fluid cytology revealed clusters of atypical cells suggestive of carcinoma with large
cell morphology. CT Chest + Abdomen (13/02/26): Bilateral pleural effusion with left sided
enhancing pleural thickening, hyperenhancing nodule in segment VIII of liver. She received
C #1 Pacliqualip + Carboplatin (20/02/26- Day 1). Patient has a history of Carcinoma
endometrium, operated 3 years ago. Scan for disease status assessment.
Labs (20/02/26): TLC – 13.88 (Neutrophil – 89.3%); INR – 2.4
Urine Routine (20/02/26): Pus cells - >100/hpf; RBC - >100/hpf; Bacteria - Present
Technique: 7.28mCi of 18F-FDG was injected intravenously and PET-CT scan was acquired from
vertex to mid-thigh after one hour. Contrast-enhanced CT scan was performed as part of the PET-
CT protocol. Breath-hold CT of the lungs was acquired as well. Standardized uptake values (SUV)
were calculated based on body weight.
FINDINGS:
Excessive motion artefacts seen- suboptimal assessment.
Brain:
Normal distribution of metabolic activity is noted intracranially. No focal area of abnormal
increased tracer avid definite lesion seen. Tiny gliotic foci seen in right ganglio-capsular
region - sequelae to ischemic insult.
Head & Neck:
Nasopharynx and hypopharynx are unremarkable.
Localized linearly increased FDG uptake is seen along the minor salivary glands in right
cheek – SUVmax 7.7 - ? inflammatory.
Intense FDG uptake noted along the inner surface of buccal mucosa- right greater than left
- SUVmax 13.85: Likely excessive physiological simulation/inflammatory.
Thyroid appears normal in size and shows no abnormal tracer uptake.
Moderate to high grade FDG avid left cervical level II, III, IV and V partly necrotic nodes
are seen. Largest - left VC node measures 2 x 2.4 cm, SUVmax 12.74
