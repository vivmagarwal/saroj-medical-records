The Anti-ENA Profile ELISA test kit provides a semiquantitative in vitro assay for human autoantibodies of the IgG class
against 8 different nuclear and cytoplasmic antigens.
1. Antinuclear antibody test is a sensitive test used to detect presence of specific autoantibodies. Antinuclear antibodies
occur in a variety of autoimmune diseases, both systemic and organ-specific.
2. The diagnosis is based primarily on the presence of compatible clinical signs and symptoms. The assay results for
autoantibodies are ancillary hence therapeutic decision, disease activity or response to treatment based solely on the result
values are not generally recommended.
