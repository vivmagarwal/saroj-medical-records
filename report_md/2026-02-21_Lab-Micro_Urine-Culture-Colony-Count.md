## Summary (extracted)

Urine Culture Colony Count (Foleys catheter sample, clinical details: CA endometrium). WET MOUNT microscopy: Pus cells 5-10/hpf, RBCs 65-70/hpf, Epithelial cells 0-5/hpf, Bacteria absent, Yeast — budding yeast cells with pseudohyphae seen. Culture grew two organisms: (1) Enterococcus faecium — Colony Count >10^5 CFU/mL (Significant bacteriuria); (2) Candida tropicalis — Colony Count >10^5 CFU/mL (Significant growth).

Drug susceptibility — Candida tropicalis (all Susceptible): Caspofungin <=0.12, Fluconazole 1.0, Micafungin <=0.06, Voriconazole <=0.12 µg/ml.

Drug susceptibility — Enterococcus faecium: Resistant to Benzylpenicillin (>=64), Ciprofloxacin (>=8), Levofloxacin (>=8), Nitrofurantoin (256), Ampicillin, Norfloxacin; Susceptible to Linezolid (2), Teicoplanin (<=0.5), Vancomycin (<=0.5), Tetracyclin (>=16); Susceptible-Dose dependent to Daptomycin (0.25). Comment notes Candida species is a common colonizer of indwelling urinary catheters; Voriconazole and Echinocandins poorly excreted in urine. Reported by Dr. Neetu Biyani and Dr. Shashikala Shivaprakasha.
